Essential Oil diffusion is the process of dispersing essential oils so that their aroma fills a room or an area up with the natural fragrance. We made our prototype to try to answer some questions we came up with, like How might we provide affordable bug spray that people are not allergic to? Our diffuser concept would answer this question by using our own natural essential oil mixture that keeps mosquitos out for people who are allergic to bug spray. The diffuser will also be small and cheap for lower income communities in warmer climates which are most affected.
