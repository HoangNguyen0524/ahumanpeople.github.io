Uganda: 1947

The Zika Virus was discovered in Uganda 1947 and it was common in Africa and Asia. The virus started to spread in 2013, but it was confirmed in 2015.

Where it's spread

The Zika Virus started in Uganda, but it's first outbreak was in 1952 and people have repotrted it in tropical Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

Microcephaly

As the virus start developing, it starts to affect child births. The virus can affect the babies head, and it's called Microcephaly. The baby is born with an abnormally small head, as their brain has not developed properly.

United States: 2015

The first ever Zika Virus outbreak in the united states started in April 2015 and the health agencies warned people of the virus and advised people to bring bug repellent.

Zika Now

Today, the Zika Virus is not relevant anymore, but it is still an important topic and in the future, there will be a cure for this Virus.
